What is IQ+?

IQ+ uses Oregon Trail-style learning to teach college-level academics at lightning speed.

🎮 How It Works

  • Every problem is interactive - click to test your thinking
  • Try again when you get it wrong (learning, not testing)
  • Process of elimination builds pattern recognition
  • Instant feedback = faster neural pathways

🧠 Why It Increases IQ

IQ+ doesn't just teach facts - it trains your brain to learn efficiently.

  • Meta-learning: Learn how to learn
  • Pattern matching: Recognize problem structures
  • Adaptive thinking: Test hypotheses in real-time
  • Mistake analysis: Understand why answers are wrong
