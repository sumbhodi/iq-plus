Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 13, 2026

The Short Version

We collect what you give us. We don't sell it. We don't track you across the web. Your data helps you learn, not advertisers target you.

What We Collect

  • Account Info: Username, email, avatar (if you upload one)
  • Learning Data: Your progress, scores, and which lessons you've completed
  • Optional Identity Info: Only if you choose to share it (preferred name, pronouns, ancestry, etc.)

What We DON'T Collect

  • Tracking cookies or pixels
  • Your browsing history outside IQ+
  • Anything we don't need for you to learn
  • Your soul (checked again, still not compatible with Firestore)

How We Use Your Data

Your data exists for exactly one purpose: helping you learn.

  • Show you your progress and scores
  • Remember where you left off
  • Let you pick up on any device
  • Build community features (optional, coming later)

Data Security

Your data is stored on Firebase (Google Cloud) with industry-standard encryption. We use authentication to ensure only you can access your account. Your optional identity info? Encrypted and private.

Your Rights

  • Access: View all your data anytime in Settings
  • Delete: Remove your account and all data whenever you want
  • Export: Download your progress data (coming soon)
  • Opt-out: You control what you share. Everything optional stays optional.

FERPA Compliance

For students under 18 or in educational settings: We comply with FERPA (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act). Your educational records are protected and never shared without consent.

Changes to This Policy

If we change this policy, we'll update the date at the top and notify you via email. Big changes? We'll ask for your consent before implementing them.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email us at privacy@iqplus.education
